Geometric Speciation

Architecture project to use part of body as inspiration to design a farctal architectural space.

The recursive geometry of a fractal, a calculated pattern that governs the formation of many natural structures, is applied to the program of an architectural space. Through multiple steps, a single flat shape is re-analyzed and multiplied to build a 3 dimensional interior and exterior experience.

Final
