XYZ Intro

[My company’s name has been changed to XYZ for privacy purposes]. XYZ is a robust senior living referral service, designed for caregivers seeking information and support as they care for aging loved ones. With their powerful mission statement “helping the helpers,” it was a joy to join the team as a Senior UX Designer.

My Role
I have been a Senior UX Designer at XYZ since July 2016. I handle all design challenges within the company including user research, product development, UI, UX, animations, and some graphic design.
About XYZ

Background

In my time at XYZ, I’ve worked primarily on internal and B2B products. One significant challenge of working here has been balancing the deep set traditions of the company with the value of modern design, especially with older users of 50+ years who may not always embrace new technology or complex flows; however, I have found this to present good design challenges. Working with the users at XYZ has allowed me to identify and compensate for my own biases, remain objective, seek deeper user research, and design with accessibility in mind.

Furthermore, as the only designer in the company, I have created a collaborative and productive environment for a variety of roles in the company, from product managers to engineers and data scientists. In working to support the needs of various groups in the company to achieve a common goal, I have broadened the scope of both my analytical and interpersonal skills, as well as learning to see design from many new perspectives.
