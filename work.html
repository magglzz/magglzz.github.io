05 TOOLBOX

Playing John Muir

Playing John Muir was a toolbox created from sheet metal and leather to store nature's most precious treasures. The box was modeled after a geode and it's pockets inspired by plants and organic matter. We wanted to create something that was not only functional, but was also an experience that related to the things the box would hold.
