Hi, I'm Margaret

I'm currently a Product Designer at

My parents were immigrants from Taipei, Taiwan, and we lived all over the world.

From NYC, to Shanghai, to Los Angeles, and to Hawaii.

Seeing all these great places influenced by their physical spaces led me to study Architecture at UCLA. But when I graduated I realized I wouldn't be able to make impact as fast as the industry would allow.

So I moved to San Francisco to join the tech community.

I started work in ad tech. Ad tech was relatively new at the time with lots of areas for improvement. So I designed an automated ad reservation system. It was my first look into product design and I was hooked.

From there I started working freelance.

I even went to Generaly Assembly to do a Front End Web Development bootcamp where I learned HTML, CSS, and Javascript (and used what I learned to make this website!)

As I grew as a designer, I wanted to dive deeper and so went to work full time at Caring.com. There I owned all aspects of design from visual, to usability, to research and everything in between.
